Vanilla Path

23/01/2024

Welcome to Vanilla World.

Hello young vanilla, welcome to the world of vanilla. This website was created with the intention of being a cheat sheet and an informal guide on how to build a website and play with your creativity in design. The project started as an assignment for the Codecademy full-stack course, but I, Vanilla Flavour, got excited, and now I will guide you, young vanillas, on the path of vanilla.

To start, please try to interact with as many things on the site as possible, hover over and see the magic happen. And don't forget, true vanilla never loses its essence.
